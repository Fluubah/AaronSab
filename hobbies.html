My Hobbies

Music

Music is my biggest hobby. I play Viola, Piano, and guitar (somewhat). I also study music theory and have been composing my own songs for a few years now.

I'm in the Austin High Orchestra, a band with my friends, and I take vocal lessons as well as viola lessons outside of school. I mainly watch youtube to learn music theory.

Video Games

Another hobby of mine is playing video games. I used to play video games a lot more and now it's more of something I do when I have nothing else to do, but I still enjoy it.

My favorite type of games are the games that make you think. Some of my favorites are Outer Wilds, The Talos Principle 1 and 2, Return of the Obra Dinn, Omori, and Stardew Valley.

Game Development

Game development is another hobby I have. I like being able to tell an interactive story being able to use my own music as well.

Game development is fun for me because it combines my passion for music, video games, and computer science all into one creative outlet where I can come up with an idea I find interesting and build something out of it
